About

When it comes to data science, I believe in gaining a breadth of knowledge in various tools, techniques, and knowledge areas. By drawing connections between different domains, I can derive new insights in areas I care about. I work with open-source and free tools to investigate public health topics, often piecing together new and disparate datasets to derive interesting conclusions. I like to spend a few weeks at a time to learn a new technical area or tool, working through tutorials with interesting new data, culminating in an informative analysis or visualization.
